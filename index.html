About

OTSUKA PAKISTAN LIMITED

Otsuka Pakistan Limited is an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007 Certified Company

About OTSUKA PAKISTAN LIMITED Otsuka Pakistan Limited was incorporated in February 1988 and commenced commercial production of intravenous solutions in September 1989 at its factory in Hub, Balochistan. OPL’s current activities are mainly focused on the manufacturing of I.V. Solutions and marketing them effectively to remain in the lead both in quality and quantity.
